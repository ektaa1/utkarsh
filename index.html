Events

Events Discription

Workshop

The UI/UX workshop is a 2-hr workshop aimed at giving the attendees an idea of what and how this field works. It will cover the basic concepts like typography, colour, hierarchy and dos and don't of UI/UX.

Picasso Pride

Picasso pride is a "Poster Presentation Competition" wherein participants can showcase their creativity and designing skills through posters.It will be a theme based competition.

Coding Competition

This competition aims to identify college students with outstanding skills in algorithms and Coding and give them an opportunity to compete on the world stage of the computer programming.

Ideathon

Ideathon is a brainstorming event in which students address a provided problem statement based on world's most pressing concerns in a short, intensive workshop-style event.

Shark Tank

Shark tank is an innovative event in which Aspiring wannabe entrepreneurs pitch their business models to a panel of judges and persuade them to approve and support their ideas by giving maximum points

Website Designing

In Web Designing contest, you will have to create a website based on the problem statement provided to you. The website will be judged on the basis of your creativity in designing the solution to the given problem.

Mock Recruitment

It is a mock placement for students to practice and develop themselves into environment of different company interviews.There will be three rounds. The students will be able to test their skills related to interviews.

Mad AD

MAD AD show is an 'Advertisement Making Competition' for students to showcase their creativity in marketing products/services. Group of students will be asked to create an advertisement or a creative campaign.
